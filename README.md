As a newly selected Student Digital Ambassador at Vellore Institute of Technology Bhopal, my core responsibility is to help my peers safely, responsibly, and confidently navigate the modern digital landscape. The transition from a guided school environment to an independent college setting requires a strong foundation in online safety, professional communication, and digital presence.

Ultimately, this portfolio acts as a personal milestone in my digital journey and a comprehensive guide to help my batchmates become secure, responsible digital citizens.

### Task 4: Professional Communication & Etiquette
Poor digital communication can severely damage a student's academic and professional reputation. To demonstrate proper etiquette, I drafted two distinct communication scenarios: a formal academic extension request to a professor and a professional internship inquiry directed to a corporate HR coordinator. Additionally, I created a rigorous Social Media Do's and Don'ts checklist to guide responsible online behavior.

### Task 5: Cybercrime Awareness & Prevention
This module focuses on safeguarding mental and emotional safety online. I conducted a comprehensive case study on Cyberbullying, detailing how the perceived anonymity of the internet allows harassment to escalate rapidly. To combat this, I developed an actionable prevention checklist that empowers students to intervene, block malicious accounts, and officially report abuse to the National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in) and the 1930 Helpline.
