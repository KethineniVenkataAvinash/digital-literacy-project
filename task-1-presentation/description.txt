Task 1: Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic

This folder, task-1-presentation/, contains the files for the first task of the Digital Literacy project. For this task, I acted as a Student Digital Ambassador to create a one-page visual resource. The main goal of this resource is to help my batchmates understand what digital literacy means and why it matters in today's world.

Inside this folder, you will find the exported image of the Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic that I designed. The infographic clearly covers several key topics required by the project. It explains the basic concept of what digital literacy is. It also highlights useful digital tools for students, shares safe internet practices, explains how to build a professional online presence, and gives tips on proper email etiquette.

This folder holds the final exported image of my design, exactly as required by the project guidelines. This visual guide is meant to be a simple, easy-to-read tool that helps students learn how to stay safe, productive, and professional on the internet.
